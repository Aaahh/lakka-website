Features

RetroArch, the software at the heart of Lakka, is an advanced frontend with some unique features. Lakka itself is built on OpenELEC, a very solid basis for an embedded system. This combination is the key of our success.

Multiplayer

You can play games up to five players.

Firmware updates

Lakka is a very compact system. The whole OS can be updated or downgraded with a single download, like modern game consoles.

Savestates

Save your progress at any moment in the game, very useful when you have to go for diner :)

Shaders

Improve the look of your old games with a vartiety of graphical filters: CRT, smoothing, scanlines and handheld borders.

Netplay

Enjoy your favorite multiplayer games over the network.

Rewind

This optionnal feature allows you to rewind your game on a simple key press.

Wireless joypads

Some wireless joypad are supported out of the box: XBOX360, Dualshock 3, and 8bitdo for more gaming comfort.

Optimizations

All our emulators are compiled with the best optimization for your particular hardware.
