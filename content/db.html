--- title: Systems ---

This game database is built using the data from No-Intro, and metadata scrapped from TheGamesDB.

No-Intro is an effort to preserve retro games by building lists of clean ROM dumps. RetroArch and Lakka rely on their work during the scanning process that builds playlists.

Boxarts are also from TheGamesDB, while titlescreens and in-game screenshots comes from Screenshot - Palace. We are maintaining all these pictures under a single github repository here.
